How to Find a Reputable Bulk SMS Provider in Canada

Posted on July 22, 2022

In the world of business, communication is important and having a way to stay in touch with your customers and suppliers is imperative. While you may not have an in-house IT department, sending bulk SMS in Canada is a low-cost solution for small businesses. However, you must find a reliable bulk SMS provider in your local area that has a good track record. There are some tips you can follow to make sure that you’re not wasting your money.

Text message marketing

If you’re thinking of getting started with bulk SMS marketing in Canada, you should know that it’s actually quite simple and cost-effective. There are a number of different text marketing software companies to choose from, so make sure to do your due diligence before committing. Many of them are overseas, which means you’ll end up paying in foreign currency, which can add as much as 30% to your costs. You should also ask about foreign currency availability and whether it’s available in Canada.

Another important factor to consider is the open rate. While email marketing can achieve a high open rate, bulk SMS is significantly more effective. According to research, 98% of texts are opened within 90 seconds, and 90% of those open messages are read within three minutes. In fact, Gartner has estimated that text messages can produce a response rate of 45%. In addition to reaching a wider audience, bulk SMS marketing in Canada is incredibly cost-effective.

Cost

You can save money on bulk SMS in Canada by choosing a service that has flat rates based on volume. You will never be charged extra for unused SMS and they will carry over to the following month. Some companies offer a Pay & Go System that allows you to top up when needed. However, you should do your due diligence before committing to any service. Keep in mind that many text marketing software companies are foreign companies, so you may have to pay fees in your local currency.

To ensure that you receive the best rates, consider a service that offers a free trial. These services typically include all carrier and passthrough fees. You can send SMS without worrying about additional charges. You will also get unlimited keywords and full access to their online platform. You can sign up for a free trial before making a decision, which means that you can try out a service with confidence before committing to it.

Quotas

While quotas for bulk SMS in Canada are often lower than those of other countries, the fact remains that bulk SMS in Canada is not supported by all operators. Operators have the right to filter, block, or restrict bulk messages. Bulk SMS is generally considered bulk SMS if it contains the same content and originator. Messages sent by fewer groups of subscribers tend to receive better delivery rates.

There are two ways to increase your outbound SMS quota. You can either opt for a dedicated Toll Free number or a multi-user account. The dedicated number comes with a quota of 500 SMS per 24 hours or 2,000 messages per calendar month. In addition, you can rent additional virtual numbers and use them for more messages. These additional numbers will automatically round robin among all subscribers.

Personalized bulk sms messages

If you want to send personalized bulk SMS messages to your customers in Canada, you need to understand CASL and send CEMs in a country with CASL. While SMS is still the most popular form of communication in Canada, it isn’t always the most effective choice. If you’re not sure whether CASL rules apply to you, check with your carrier.

In general, bulk texting is simple and straightforward. In Canada, you can send messages to a wide range of subscribers in a matter of minutes. You can also schedule your messages to be sent at future dates and times, and you can even send these text messages from a different computer. Once you’ve gotten your list, you can begin sending mass texts! The best way to use SMS marketing is to find the sweet spot between being annoying and being helpful.
